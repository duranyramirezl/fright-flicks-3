Episode 5: The Lighthouse:

"Monochrome Lovecraftian Horror"

Director: Robert Eggers

Genres: Psychological-Horror, Thriller, Black-and-white

The Lighthouse thrives on its stark black-and-white cinematography, which creates a claustrophobic, timeless atmosphere. The harsh lighting and use of shadows enhance the descent into madness, and the special effects, from the stormy sea to the mysterious mermaid, are subtle yet haunting. Symbolism runs rampant, from the towering lighthouse as a symbol of unattainable knowledge to the recurring imagery of birds and the sea, reflecting the characters' psychological unraveling.
