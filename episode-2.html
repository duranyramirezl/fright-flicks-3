Episode 2: Pearl:

"The Glamour of Madness"

Director: Ti West

Genres: Horror, Slasher, Thriller

Pearl stands out with its vibrant, almost dreamlike technicolor visuals, which sharply contrast the dark psychological underpinnings of the film. The cinematography mirrors classic Hollywood, with heightened colors and surrealism, enhancing Pearl's descent into madness. The practical effects and striking symbolism—particularly the use of mirrors and reflections—deepen the viewer's understanding of Pearl's fractured identity.
